ABOUT ORENOHIBI

Orenohibi is an online recipe site for beverages that could be made at home. Each beverage has been specifically designed in a way that it is affordable and tasty.

Orenohibiは緻密なレシピエンジニアリングによって開発されたレシピをWebサイト上で公開しております。全ての人にわかりやすく、幸せなおうち時間をお約束します。


OUR SPIRITS

Accessible Ingredients With Best Potential


Orenohibi introduces how to create high quality beverages using day to day items and ingredients that are available in your local supermarket. With the spread of covid19 it has become much harder to drop in a cafe. By using Orenohibi’s well throughout recipes, we believe people can brew their own beverages with a guided hand, and thus help them emulate the cafe experience at their homes.

「全ての人に、幸せなひとときを。」Orenohibiの根幹です。昨今のコロナの事情によって思うように行動ができなくなった中、それでも幸せを支えたい。全ての人に簡単に手に入る材料を使ったドリンクレシピを公開しています。緻密なレシピエンジニアリングは全ての人にわかりやすく、幸せなおうち時間をお約束します。
